About

We have over 20 years of experience in the building industry, we are able to confidently offer all types of plumbing and gas-fitting services. Not to mention full bathroom renovations.

We have extensive experience in residential new builds, bathroom renovations, plumbing/gas maintenance and unit blocks.

Jared Rayner and the team at Hydrospark Plumbing have the energy, the experience and the enthusiasm to get the job done with good service and competitive prices.
We love buildingstrong relationships with our returning customers. Originally based in Sydney and since 2017 have been also operating in the Cairns region building up a great client base.

You will be amazed with the reliability and service that we offer. Hydrospark Plumbing is fully insured and offer warranty on all our workmanship
